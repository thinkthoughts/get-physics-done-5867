# Key Equations

## Signal Model

d(t) = h(t) + n(t)

---

## Fourier Transform

H(f) = ∫ d(t) e^{-i 2π f t} dt

---

## Signal-to-Noise Ratio (SNR)

SNR ~ |H(f)|^2 / S_n(f)

---

## Interpretation

Detection requires identifying peaks in frequency space that persist over time and exceed noise thresholds.
